A cool refreshing glass of Lassi

In this recipe, you will be taught how to make a glass of Lassi to quench your thirst.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add ingredients in a blender and blend until no longer icy. If you would like to keep some of the bits of ice, blend for a shorter time.
  2. Taste and add sugar for sweetness, yogurt for more tart flavor, ice to make it cooler.
  3. Pour into two large glasses or 3-4 small. This recipe makes around 4 cups (32 oz), so 2 large lassis or 4 kid-size, though my kids usually demand more than 8 oz.
  4. And that's it! It wasn't that hard but it sure tastes amazing doesn't it!
